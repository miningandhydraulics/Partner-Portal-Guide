Overview
--------

The MHS Partner Portal is your gateway to:

- **Search and order parts** from our extensive inventory
- **Track your orders** in real-time
- **Access your account information** and order history
- **Manage custom part numbers** for your specific needs
- **View real-time stock levels** from our Perth warehouse and suppliers

System Requirements
-------------------

The portal works on:

- **Desktop browsers**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
- **Mobile devices**: iOS Safari, Android Chrome
- **Tablets**: All modern tablet browsers

.. note::
   For the best experience, we recommend using a desktop or laptop computer when placing large orders or using advanced features.

What You'll Need
----------------

To get started, you'll need:

1. **Your login credentials** (provided by MHS)
2. **Your company information** (for account setup)
3. **Internet connection** (for real-time stock updates)
